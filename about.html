AI Kaduna Saturday

Date: Saturday, April 6, 2024
Time: 1:00 PM – 4:00 PM WAT
Location: KADA Hive, No. 11B Sambo Road, Kaduna

About the Event

“AI Kaduna Saturday” is a community-led technical workshop aimed at helping developers—from beginners to seasoned professionals—learn how to build and apply generative AI solutions. Hosted by GDG Kaduna City, the event spotlights tools like Google’s Gemini, Vertex AI, and Duet AI, and brings together backend, mobile, web, and Flutter developers for hands-on learning.

Event Details

  • What to Expect: Intro to Gen‑AI, panel discussions, live code labs, networking.
  • Who Should Attend: Developers (backend, Android, Flutter, web), AI/ML enthusiasts, students.
  • Format: Mix of presentations and hands-on labs using Gemini in Python, web, or Flutter.
